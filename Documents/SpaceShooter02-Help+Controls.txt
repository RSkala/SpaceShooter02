HELP / HOW TO PLAY

Gamepad Controls:
* Move ship with Right Thumbstick
* Aim and fire with Left Thumbstick
* Boost forward with Right Bumper

Mouse & Keyboard Controls:
* Move ship with WASD
* Aim and fire using mouse cursor and left click
* Boost forward with Space Bar

Instructions:
* Enemies will often drop Score Multiplier item
* Picking up Score Multipliers will refill meter
* Meter will drain once full
* Pickup Score Multipliers to partially refill meter.
* Boost refills over time
* Player is invincible while Boosting!
